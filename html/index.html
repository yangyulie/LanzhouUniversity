Announcement

Dates: 16-18 October 2019
Venue: Lanzhou University, Lanzhou, China

We are pleased to announce that the International Workshop on Chemistry-Climate Interactions will take place October 16-18, 2019 at the campus of Lanzhou University (LZU), China. The workshop will focus on the interactions between climate and chemical composition both in the stratosphere and troposphere. The workshop aims to highlight current research activities and provide an opportunity for researchers, experts and students from China and other countries to share their knowledge and expertise.

Scientific themes
Dynamical and chemical coupling of the stratosphere and troposphere;
Circulation and transport in the UTLS, including stratosphere-troposphere exchange;
Air quality and aerosol behavior in China and their climate impact.
